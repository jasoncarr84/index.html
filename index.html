About Ask

Ask searches Amazon for you and shows a clear top pick, with real prices, ratings, and reviews pulled live at the moment you search.

How it's funded

When you tap "View on Amazon" and buy something, Amazon pays us a small commission through their Associates affiliate program. This never changes the price you pay — it's the same price either way.

A few honest notes

Prices, ratings, and stock can change quickly on Amazon. What you see here reflects the moment you searched, and may occasionally be slightly out of date or, rarely, wrong.

Products are sold and shipped by Amazon or its third-party sellers, not by us. Any purchase, return, or shipping issue is between you and Amazon, under Amazon's own terms.

Your information

This app doesn't require an account, and doesn't collect or store any personal information about you. Your search history is kept only on your own device.
